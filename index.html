Li Zhou

Senior Machine Learning Engineer

Twitter, Inc.

I am now working on Machine Learning at Twitter. Before this, I received my PhD from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Ohio State University, working with Prof. Radu Teodorescu. My research interests lie in the broad area of computer architecture/system, large-scale graph processing, distributed machine learning, and security.
